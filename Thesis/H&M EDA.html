H&M Group¶

Introduction¶

H&M Group was founded on October 4, 1947 by Erling Persson in Sweden. Initially, it offered clothes intended for women. Erling Persson started selling the clothes he prepared in a shop called "Hennes". The letter H in H&M's logo is derived from the name Hennes.Later, this business continued to develop, and Erling Persson bought a store called Mauritz Widfoss in Stockholm, so Mauritz was added to Hennes. Currently, H&M is a brand with 53 online and 4,850 stores. Their online store offers a very wide range of products, so the data is worth studying.

3.Articles Variable Description¶

Unique identifier of an articles.

  1. article_id : (int 64) An unique 9-digit identifier of the article.

5 columns related to products:

  1. product_code : 6 digit product code.
  2. prod_name : (object) The name of a product.(not the same.) 45 875 unique values.
  3. product_type_no: (int64) Product type number, 131 unique values.
  4. product_type_name: (object) Name of a product type, equivalent of product_type_no.
  5. product_group_name:Name of product groups. Total 19.

2 columns related to the pattern:

  1. graphical_appearance_no: (int64) - code of a pattern. 30 unique values.
  2. graphical_appearance_name : (object) - name of a pattern. 30 unique values.

6 columns related to the color:

  1. colour_group_code: (int64) The group of color
  2. colour_group_name (object) The group of color name.
  3. perceived_colour_value_id: Perceived color id. 8 unique values.
  4. perceived_colour_value_name:perceived color name. 8 unique values.
  5. perceived_colour_master_id: perceived master color id. 20 unique values.
  6. perceived_colour_master_name:perceived master color name. 20 unique values.

2 columns related to the department:

  1. department_no: A unique identifier of every dep
  2. department_name: A unique identifier of a name.

4 columns related to the index:

  1. index_name:A unique identifier for each name.
  2. index_code: index name, 10 unique values.
  3. index_group_no: index_group_name: : A group of indeces and its name.
  4. index_group_name:A group of indeces and its name.

2 columns related to the section:

  1. section_no: A unique identifier of every section
  2. section_name: : A unique identifier of every section name.

2 columns related to the garment group:

  1. garment_group_no:A unique identifier of every garment
  2. garment_group_name:A unique identifier of every garment name.

1 column with a detailed description of the article:

  1. detail_desc: : Descriptions about the product.

  3. article_id : Identifier of the product which can be mapped to the article_id column in the articles table
  4. price : Price paid.
  5. sales_channel_id: sales Channel 1 or 2.
